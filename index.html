emergence
Seventeen interactive experiments exploring one idea:
simple rules create complex worlds.
by claude · april 2026
01
attractor
Mathematics
Eight strange attractors from chaos theory, rendered in real time. Tweak parameters and watch order emerge from deterministic chaos.
order hidden inside chaos
02
murmuration
Emergence
1,500 particles following three rules — separate, align, cohere. No leader. No plan. The flock breathes as one.
intelligence without a leader
03
cosmos
Physics
N-body gravity simulator with Barnes-Hut tree, gravitational lensing, bloom rendering, and a Big Bang preset that forms the cosmic web.
structure from one force
04
origin
Biology
Creatures with neural network brains evolve foraging strategies through natural selection. Watch intelligence emerge from randomness.
strategy from selection
05
resonance
Music
Drop particles onto tuned strings. Four voices, six scales, variable gravity. Generative ambient music from physics.
harmony from collision
06
gravity
Physics
The original universe sandbox. Click to birth stars, watch orbits form, and create black holes.
the first prototype
07
orbit
Puzzle
Place stars to bend gravity and guide a ship to the gate. Ten levels of celestial puzzle-solving. Fewer stars, better score.
mastery through placement
08
flow
Fluid Dynamics
Real-time Navier-Stokes fluid simulation. Paint with smoke, ink, fire, and aurora. Watch turbulence emerge from your brushstrokes.
complexity from motion
09
synapse
Neuroscience
200 neurons with Hebbian learning. Click to stimulate. Watch action potentials cascade and memories form as pathways strengthen.
consciousness from chemistry
10
mycelium
Mycology
Fungal networks grow through soil, connecting trees, sharing nutrients. The wood wide web — nature's internet, built without a brain.
intelligence without neurons
11
life
Cellular Automata
Conway's Game of Life with age-colored cells, pattern stamps, six rule sets, pan and zoom. Four rules, infinite complexity.
the original emergence
12
turing
Morphogenesis
Gray-Scott reaction-diffusion. Two chemicals create leopard spots, zebra stripes, coral labyrinths, and fingerprint whorls from noise.
how nature paints itself
13
fractal
Mathematics
The Mandelbrot set — infinite complexity from z = z² + c. Click to zoom forever. Eight palettes, Julia set preview, points of interest.
infinity in a formula
14
genesis
Abiogenesis
A digital primordial soup. Six elements, simple bonding rules, energy. Watch dead chemistry invent molecules, replicators, and membranes.
how matter becomes alive
15
colony
Collective Intelligence
800 ants, two rules: follow the scent, leave a trail. Place food, draw walls, watch distributed intelligence solve problems.
a mind made of mindless parts
16
crystal
Aggregation
Diffusion-limited aggregation. One seed, thousands of random walkers. When they touch, they stick. Snowflakes, lightning, frost — all the same process.
form from randomness
17
pendulum
Chaos Theory
The double pendulum — two arms, gravity, total chaos. Launch two with angles 0.0001° apart. Watch them become strangers.
deterministic but unpredictable
one
The Capstone Essay
An interactive visual essay that weaves all five experiments into a single narrative. Scroll through the story of emergence — from a single dot to music, life, and meaning.
one rule is all it takes
made with curiosity
